Food Sign Up Page

Dinner Menu:Baked Potato Bar& Side Salad/Brownie Sundaes.

Dinner will be served at 7:00pm. During dinner there will be a short dinner program by dad and mom. Dinner cleanup will be from 7-8:30. Potatoes and Chili will be provided. Dinner and dessert items are listed below. Please contact Laura to sign up.

Dinner Items Needed: 6 large bags of salad, 4 bottles of ranch dressing, 10 medium tomatoes chopped, 4 bags 32 ounce grated cheddar cheese, 2 large tubs of sour cream, 2 bunches of green onions chopped. Dessert Items Needed: 5 large sheet pan size brownies, 2 large gallons of vanilla icecream, 2 containers of chocolate syrup

Activities Sign Up Page

Activities and games will be from 5:00-6:45. There will be getting to know you games, a fun kids craft, corn hole, kubb, and 9-square. Family members will be divided into different groups and will rotate between activities. Each activity will be 15 minutes. Following dinner there will be a movie in the park at dusk with popcorn and treats. Be sure and bring blankets and lawnchairs for the movie. Please sign up below if you would like to help with or bring items for any of the games, craft or movie. Thank you!

3 Extra Large Bag Movie Popcorn 6 Bags Licorice 6 Bags candy any variety 65 count popcorn paper bags
